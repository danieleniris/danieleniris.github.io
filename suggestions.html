Fiss



We shall be staying in the town off Fiss, which caters for nice walks, bars, and skiing. There are a few places of special interest to us, which we list below:

This is where the party will be.

Hotel Bergfrieden


And part of you are staying in Hotel Bergfrieden, (Laurschweg 5, 6533 Fiss, Austria).


Travel

Fiss can be reached by plane (Salzburg, Innsbruck, or Munich), train (Landeck), and by car. When travelling by plane, we would recommend to travel from the airport to Fiss by train or airport transfer. Alternatively, you can arrange amongst the other guest for a car-share or airport pickup.
