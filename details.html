Dresscode

We are easy going and are not strongly wedded to traditional styles. But we do like dressing up (a bit)! The theme to our wedding and wedding ceremony will be retro skiing (or in Dutch: foute skipak), for the welcome reception and dinner on Sunday the theme will be smart casual.

In particular we like to theme-up for the ceremony, photo on Saturday and cake and toast on Sunday. For the dinner on Saturday and party the dresscode is either retroski outfit or smart casaul, whatever you feel most comfortable in.

For the wedding party and dinner on Saturday, we recommend something comfortable and smart casual that you can fit under your retro ski suit (or your ski suit itself!). Go wild and find your best terrible retro/vintage skisuit! For inspiration, see this neat fella here --> (or up ^, whereever he's floated). You can find options on eBay, Marktplaats, or vinted.nl (search: vintage retro skiing suit). It has not been possible for us to arrange a bulk rental of skisuits.
