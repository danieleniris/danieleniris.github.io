Tupperware car

This is an embarassingly simple arduino car. All you really need is

  • 2 9V batteries
  • 2 BYJ48 steppermotors
  • 2 regular wheels
  • 1 uniwheel
  • 1 tupperware box

All you need to do is hook it all up and you are done!

...Well, not that simple unfortunately. As it turns out, steppermotors are not really made to cranck at high speeds. As a result the native stepperlibraries and examples with the arduino don't work well. Max you get those to turn is around 4rpm. That's centuries. You need to use a separate library, like this one: . Now, the speed is still not great, up to 15rpm, but now your car will creep forward at last. You want to have it go faster, well don't use steppers!
